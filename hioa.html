Branding

2023

Hioa is an innovative flower brand that aims to transform the traditional flower shop experience, focuses on creating a vibrant and modern brand identity that resonates with a new generation of customers. With an emphasis on emotional connection, Hioa offers more than just flowers; we deliver unique and joyful experiences that celebrate life's moments. Our carefully curated arrangements, known as "boxes of love and happiness," are made to bring a burst of energy and positivity to any occasion.
